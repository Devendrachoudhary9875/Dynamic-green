Environmental analysis is a strategic tool.
It is a process to identify all the external and internal elements, which can affect the organization's performance.
The analysis entails assessing the level of threat or opportunity the factors might present.

The factors and conditions (such as economic, legal, political, and social circumstances) that generally affect everyone in an industry or market in more or less similar manner.

(strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats)
SWOT analysis is one of the most popular strategic analysis models.
It involves looking at the strengths and weaknesses of your business' capabilities, and any opportunities and threats to your business.
  • capitalise on your strengths
  • minimise the effects of your weaknesses
  • make the most of any opportunities
  • reduce the impact of any threats
